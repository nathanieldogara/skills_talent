ABOUT SKILLS & TALENT (STA)

Since its inception, Skills and Talent Arena (STA) has a mission to empower every vulnerable youth and other community members to become self-reliant and able to effectively contribute socio-economically towards community development. We’re optimistic and strongly believe that, if we work together we believe we can empower vulnerable youths and other community members with opportunities that can take that above their circumstances. We know opportunities exist everywhere, and so too is greatness. To achieve our mission, STA designs and carries out projects that support vulnerable youths, Internally Displaced Persons, prisoners, orphans, widows, and other needy populations within our community.

Our Mission

We are an organization dedicated to accompanying vulnerable youths and other community members to become self-reliant and able to effectively contribute socio-economically to community development.

Our Vision

We want to see communities where vulnerable youths and other community members are empowered for socio-economic development at both personal and community levels.

Achievement

We have Direct Beneficiaries of over 3900K, Indirect Beneficiaries of over 300M, Served over 36 communities and over 21 ongoing projects
